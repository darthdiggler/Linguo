:Por and Para:
por
1. Motion (through, along, by) 
2. Cause or motive of an action (because of, on account of, on behalf of ) 
3. Agency, means, manner, unit of measure (by, for, per)
4. In exchange for 
5. Period of time during which an action takes place (during, in, for) 

para
1. Destination in space (to)2
2. Goal for a point in the future (by, for) 
3. Whom or what something is for 
4. Purpose (in order to)
